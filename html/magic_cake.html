Magic Cake is one of the testiest cakers the world has never known. few have it and lived to tell about it, and there are thir stories

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non- stick dish
  3. bake at 350 degress for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temperatura for 20 minutes
  5. eat and enjoy
